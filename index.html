Introduction

Welcome to One Stroke, a puzzle game developed by CS Boiii. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the One Stroke mobile application ("App"), available on the Google Play Store.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

✅
Short version: We don't collect your personal data. The App is designed to work without any user accounts or personal information.
2
Information We Do NOT Collect

One Stroke is built with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any of the following:

❌ Name ❌ Email Address ❌ Phone Number ❌ Location Data ❌ Device Identifiers ❌ Photos / Camera ❌ Contacts ❌ Microphone ❌ Biometric Data ❌ Payment Information ❌ Usage Profiles ❌ IP Address (by us)

There is no backend server operated by CS Boiii that receives or stores any information from your device.

3
Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves certain game data locally on your device only to provide a smooth experience. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us.

Data Type Purpose Stored Where
Level progress Remember which levels you completed Device local storage (Hive / SharedPreferences)
Streak count Track your daily play streak Device local storage
Badges earned Display your achievements Device local storage
Game settings Sound on/off, preferences Device local storage
Daily challenge state Track daily challenge completion Device local storage
🔒
100% Local: All the above data is stored exclusively on your device using standard Android local storage. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all this data.
4
Advertising — Google AdMob

One Stroke is a free-to-play game supported by advertisements served through Google AdMob. AdMob is a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC.

The following types of ads may be displayed:

📺 Banner Ads 🎬 Interstitial Ads 🎁 Rewarded Ads

What AdMob may collect independently:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • General location (country/region level, not precise GPS)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • IP address (used for general location and fraud prevention)
⚠️
Important: This data is collected directly by Google AdMob, not by CS Boiii. We have no access to this data. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for full details on how AdMob handles your information.

You can learn more and opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads Opt-Out
  • On your Android device: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Data Security

Since One Stroke does not collect or transmit personal data to any server operated by us, the risk of a data breach from our end is eliminated by design.

All game data (progress, settings, streaks) is stored exclusively on your local device and protected by your device's own security mechanisms (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

🛡️
Zero-server architecture: There is no CS Boiii server that stores your data. No server breach can ever expose your information to us, because we never receive it in the first place.

Your Rights & Choices

Even though we don't collect personal data, you still have full control over your experience:

  • Delete local data: Uninstall the App to permanently remove all locally stored game data from your device.
  • Ad preferences: Opt out of personalized ads via your Android device settings under Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  • Reset Advertising ID: You can reset your Google Advertising ID at any time via Android Settings.
  • Contact us: For any privacy-related questions or concerns, reach out to us at the contact details below.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. Since we do not process your personal data, most such rights are satisfied by default. For any specific requests, contact us.
